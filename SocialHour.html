How does it work?

  • Choose the number of tables and number of people per table (up to 5).
  • Choose your list of table topics. You can start with our suggestions or provide your own.
  • Send the event link to your guests and watch as people arrive in the lobby.
  • Guests choose their tables, and then are prompted to shuffle around every so often.
  • Anyone can set the music for the event and change volume (use headphones to avoid echo!)
  • Anyone can make an announcement to the whole group at any time.
  • At the end of the event, thank your guests for coming and send everyone on their way.

Questions

How many guests can I invite?

We support a maximum of 10 tables with 5 guests each.

What browsers are supported?

For the best experience on the desktop, try Chrome. Edge, Safari, and Firefox should also be okay. On the phone, use Safari on iOS, and Chrome on Android.

How much does it cost?

It's free while we're in beta. Custom plans with more features and configurability are in the works.

How secure is it?

We built Social Hour as a fun tool to power social events for work, family, and friends. Should be fine for that purpose, but maybe don't discuss state secrets here!
